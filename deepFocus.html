This is my Deep Focus Page

The Concept of Deep Focus is very important for me, As I am Very easily distracted. The Name comes from a Spotify Playlist, which I Often Use to Work when I am in a loud surroundings.

Here are some useful Tools I use:

  • Deepl is like google Translate but much better
  • Anki is a Flashcard App, which I use alot due to my hit & miss memory. Its available as a Webapp, or an App for Computer & Phone and you can even share & find Flashcard decks on this website
